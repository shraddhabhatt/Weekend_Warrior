Academy Overview


gameWARRIORS is a brand-new one of the premier and most renowned soccer clubs in the world, and Grande Sports Academy, one of the most successful youth soccer development academies in the United States. gameWARRIORS is the only U.S. Soccer Development Academy program in the United States with a full-time residential program partnered with a top international professional team. Academy players will have access to the state-of-the-art Grande Sports World facility with on-site dorms, on-site soccer, nutrition, and training facilities, and an on-site high school overseen by one of the finest universities in the nation, Arizona State University. It is the ideal place for players to develop on the field, in the classroom, and as a person among the most elite youth soccer players from the United States and around the world.
